Our Story

Made with Soul
in Brooklyn

Sweet Borough started as a kitchen experiment in 2019 — a way to share the flavors of home with neighbors during the holidays. Word spread block by block, and what began as a hobby became a calling.

Today, we craft every confection by hand using no artificial additives, no shortcuts, and no compromise. Every batch is small. Every bite is intentional. Every box carries a little piece of Brooklyn sweetness.
